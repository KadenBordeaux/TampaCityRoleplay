The Master Sheet

Documentation Overview

This portal serves as the official repository for all Tampa City Roleplay rules, guidelines, and departmental protocols. Navigating through the sidebar will provide you with the necessary information to maintain the high standard of roleplay expected in our community.

Please Note: Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for breaking them. Ensure you stay updated with the latest patch notes.

Policy 1.0

Discord Rules

1.1 Community Respect

Treat all members with respect. No toxicity, harassment, or hate speech will be tolerated under any circumstances.

1.2 Content Standards

TCRP is a strictly SFW community. Posting NSFW content, links, or imagery will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

Policy 2.0

Game Rules

2.1 FearRP

You must value your life. If you are held at gunpoint or significantly outmatched, you must comply with the aggressor as if your life were truly at stake.

2.2 Meta-Gaming

Using information gathered outside of character (OOC) for in-character (IC) benefit is strictly prohibited and punishable by staff.
